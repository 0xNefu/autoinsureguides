# Pennsylvania Car Insurance Guide 2026

Pennsylvania drivers pay an average of **,300/year** for car insurance, but your actual rate depends on your driving history, location, and coverage choices.

## Pennsylvania Minimum Coverage Requirements
- **Bodily Injury Liability**: $25,000 per person / $50,000 per accident
- **Property Damage Liability**: $50,000 per accident
- **Additional requirements may apply** - check with local providers

## How to Save on Car Insurance in Pennsylvania

### 1. Take Advantage of PA-Specific Discounts
- Ask about state-specific safe driver programs
- Check for low-mileage discounts if you drive less than average
- Inquire about defensive driving course discounts

### 2. Compare Quotes Regularly
- Rates change every 6-12 months
- Get at least 3 quotes before renewing
- Consider both national and regional insurers

### 3. Optimize Your Coverage
- Adjust deductibles based on your financial situation
- Remove unnecessary coverage for older vehicles
- Consider usage-based insurance if you drive less

## Top Insurance Companies in Pennsylvania
