## How to Save Money on Coverage

1. **Increase your deductible** - Can lower premiums 15-30%
2. **Bundle policies** - Home + auto discounts
3. **Maintain good credit** - Impacts rates in most states
4. **Ask about discounts** - Safe driver, student, military

## When to Consider Additional Coverage

- If you have a new or expensive vehicle
- If you have significant assets to protect
- If you frequently commute in heavy traffic
- If you have teenage drivers
